  • Title: Create balanced exam sets

Our goal is to design a series of well-balanced exams featuring diverse questions while ensuring the fairness and comparability of evaluations. This is particularly crucial in large-scale applications where numerous organizations administer varied tests, regularly updating questions. In this context, our solution guarantees that the difficulty levels of multiple tests remain mathematically equal, fostering equitable evaluation practices.
